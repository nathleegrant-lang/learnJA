Platform features

Everything a student needs. Nothing more.

Built specifically for Jamaican primary school children — culturally relevant, fully automated, and genuinely fun.

🔊
Natural text-to-speech
Every word, sentence and lesson is narrated in a warm, natural voice. Tap any word to hear it. Perfect for early readers in Grades 1–2.
🌐
3D interactive explorer
Rotate the human body, fly through the solar system, explore Jamaica's parishes and manipulate 3D shapes — all in the browser.
🤖
AI feedback engine
Explains why an answer is right or wrong, not just the score. Voiced in friendly Jamaican English. Encourages without discouraging.
📅
Auto-assigned tasks
The AI assigns the right lesson at the right time. Spaced repetition automatically brings back topics the student needs to review.
🏅
Gamified progress
Earn Jamaican Stars, unlock badges like "Mathlete" and "Science Explorer," and collect Doctor Bird stickers for streaks.
📊
Parent progress reports
Automated weekly reports sent to parents by email or WhatsApp — lessons done, scores, badges earned and what to focus on next.
